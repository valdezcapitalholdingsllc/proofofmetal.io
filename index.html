THESIS

Why Proof Of Metal exists

Proof Of Metal exists to track the intersection of physical bullion and digital financial infrastructure. Not every tokenized metal product carries the same transparency, redemption rights, custody clarity, or market behavior.

This dashboard helps users compare tokenized metals against spot benchmarks while also judging whether those digital claims appear robust, usable, and credible.
